About Me

Hey! I am currently R&D Engineer at Inria rennes with Prof. Julien Pettre , team RAINBOW . I have wonderful time in Ecole Centrale de Lyon, with Prof. Liming Chen and Dr. Emmanuel Dellandréa on FUI project PIKAFLEX .

My current resarch is on Crowd tracking and crowd simulation. I have worked on different computer vision problems such as image segmentaiton, multiple object tracking, object localication and recognition, semantic image segmentation as well as 3D object recognition based on point cloud and domain adaptation.

During My PhD studies, I worked on the unsupervised image segmentation and multiple objects tracking with Prof. Simon Masnou and Prof. Liming Chen. I completed my M.E. and B.S. majored in Biomedical Engineering in School of Geosciences and Info-physics from Central South University, Changsha, China. I was working on the medical image processing, liver CT image segmentation with Prof. Yuqian Zhao
